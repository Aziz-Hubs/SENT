### **A. Corporate Operations (The Internal Users)**

_How the HQ departments use SENT's own software ("Dogfooding")._

#### **1\. Executive Office (CEO & Strategy)**

- **Function:** Strategic direction, High-level KPI monitoring.
- **Powered By:**
  - **SENTprism** (from **SENTerp**): For real-time company-wide dashboards.
  - **SENThorizon** (from **SENTmsp**): For strategic technology roadmapping.

#### **2\. Internal Human Resources (HR)**

- **Function:** Talent acquisition, Employee records, Internal Payroll, Culture.
- **Powered By:**
  - **SENTpeople** (from **SENTerp**): Used to manage SENT's own employees.
  - **SENTvault** (from **SENTerp**): Used to store secure employee contracts.
  - **SENTscribe** (from **SENTerp**): Used for the Employee Handbook (Wiki).
  - **SENTchat** (from **SENTerp**): Used for internal team communication.

#### **3\. Internal Finance & Legal**

- **Function:** Corporate accounting, Tax filing (JoFotara), Contract management.
- **Powered By:**
  - **SENTcapital** (from **SENTerp**): The central ledger for SENT LLC.
  - **SENTbridge** (from **SENTerp**): For filing SENT's own taxes with JoFotara.
  - **SENTvault** (from **SENTerp**): Replaces SharePoint for legal contracts.
  - **SENTsheet** (from **SENTerp**): For ad-hoc financial modeling.

#### **4\. Marketing & Growth**

- **Function:** Managing the sent.jo website, Lead generation, Email campaigns.
- **Powered By:**
  - **SENTcanvas** (from **SENTerp**): Used to build the public corporate website.
  - **SENTorbit** (from **SENTerp**): Used to track inbound leads.
  - **SENTdeck** (from **SENTerp**): Used to create sales pitch decks.
  - **SENTcontrol** (from **SENTmsp**): Used to manage marketing SaaS subscriptions.

#### **5\. Internal IT & Security (The CISO Office)**

- **Function:** Securing SENT's own infrastructure and staff.
- **Powered By:**
  - **SENTpulse** (from **SENTmsp**): Monitors SENT's own employee laptops.
  - **SENTradar** (from **SENTsec**): Monitors SENT's internal logs.
  - **SENTmind** (from **SENTsec**): Security awareness training for staff.
  - **SENTnexus** (from **SENTmsp**): Stores internal IT passwords and maps.
